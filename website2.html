Famous Temples

Temple of Angkor Wat

Located in Cambodia, Angkor Wat is the largest religious monument in the world and a UNESCO World Heritage site.

The Golden Temple

Also known as Harmandir Sahib, the Golden Temple in Amritsar, India is the holiest site in Sikhism.

Shwedagon Pagoda

This ancient Buddhist temple is located in Yangon, Myanmar, and is one of the most iconic structures in the country.
